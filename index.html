Our Donate Plan

There are different plans for meeting their basic needs so they can get a life they should have.

Food is the most essential need for daily life. So at first we are trying to ensure healthy food for helpless people. They won't be dying in hunger anymore.

Shelter for a family

Many people live in unusual places because they don't have We are trying to make shelters where homeless people can live . They will not have to live in unhealthy places in severe weather anymore.

With education there children can make a better future. When they are educated they will not have to wait for our help anymore. We will also introduce vocational course for older people so they can find a work.
